About Healthcare

Healthcare is the maintenance or improvement of health via the prevention, diagnosis, treatment, amelioration or cure of disease, illness, injury, and other physical and mental impairments in people.

The future of healthcare is uncertain, but there are many opportunities for innovation. By addressing the challenges facing healthcare systems today, we can ensure that everyone has access to high-quality, affordable healthcare in the future.
